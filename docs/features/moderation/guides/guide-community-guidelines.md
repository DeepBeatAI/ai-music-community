# Community Guidelines

## Welcome to the AI Music Community Platform

Our platform is built on creativity, collaboration, and respect. These Community Guidelines help ensure a positive experience for all users. By using the platform, you agree to follow these guidelines.

## Core Principles

### 1. Respect and Kindness

Treat all community members with respect and kindness. We celebrate diversity and welcome creators from all backgrounds.

### 2. Authenticity and Transparency

Be honest about your content, especially regarding AI-generated music. Proper disclosure and attribution are essential.

### 3. Safety First

Help us maintain a safe environment by reporting violations and avoiding harmful content.

### 4. Creative Expression

We encourage creative expression while maintaining community standards. Push boundaries responsibly.

## AI-Generated Content Requirements

### Mandatory Disclosure

When uploading AI-generated music, you **must**:

1. **Clearly indicate** that the content is AI-generated
2. **Specify the AI tool** used to create the music
3. **Provide accurate metadata** about the generation process
4. **Respect copyright** of any training data or samples

### Best Practices

- Be transparent about your creative process
- Credit AI tools and any human collaborators
- Distinguish between AI-assisted and fully AI-generated content
- Respect the intellectual property of others

### What's Not Allowed

- Claiming AI-generated content as entirely human-created
- Misrepresenting the AI tool used
- Using AI to copy or imitate specific artists without permission
- Generating content that violates copyright

## Enforcement and Consequences

### Violation Severity Levels

#### Minor Violations
- First-time spam
- Unintentional guideline violations
- Minor inappropriate content

**Typical Actions:**
- Warning notification
- Content removal
- Educational guidance

#### Moderate Violations
- Repeated spam
- Harassment or bullying
- Copyright violations
- Inappropriate content

**Typical Actions:**
- Content removal
- Temporary restrictions (posting, commenting, or uploading disabled)
- Temporary suspension (1-7 days)
- Warning with explanation

#### Severe Violations
- Hate speech
- Serious harassment
- Impersonation
- Repeated violations after warnings

**Typical Actions:**
- Content removal
- Extended suspension (7-30 days)
- Multiple restrictions applied
- Account review

#### Critical Violations
- Self-harm promotion
- Threats of violence
- Illegal activities
- Severe or repeated violations

**Typical Actions:**
- Immediate content removal
- Account suspension or ban
- Report to authorities (if applicable)
- Permanent platform removal

### Moderation Actions

Moderators may take the following actions:

1. **Content Removal**: Permanently delete violating content
2. **Content Hidden**: Temporarily hide content during review
3. **User Warning**: Notify user of guideline violation
4. **Posting Disabled**: Prevent user from creating new posts
5. **Commenting Disabled**: Prevent user from commenting
6. **Upload Disabled**: Prevent user from uploading tracks
7. **Temporary Suspension**: Suspend account for 1, 7, or 30 days
8. **Permanent Ban**: Permanently remove user from platform (admin only)

### Appeal Process

If you believe a moderation action was taken in error:

1. **Review the Guidelines**: Ensure you understand what was violated
2. **Check Your Notification**: Read the reason provided for the action
3. **Contact Support**: Reach out through the platform's help center
4. **Provide Context**: Explain why you believe the action was incorrect
5. **Be Patient**: Appeals are reviewed carefully and may take time

**Note:** The appeal process is currently being developed. Check back for updates.

## Reporting Violations

### How to Report

If you encounter content that violates these guidelines:

1. Click the **report button (üö©)** on the content
2. Select the appropriate violation category
3. Provide additional context if needed
4. Submit your report

For detailed reporting instructions, see the [User Reporting Guide](guide-user-reporting.md).

### What to Report

Report content that:
- Clearly violates community guidelines
- Poses a safety risk to users
- Contains illegal content
- Involves harassment or threats

### What Not to Report

Don't report content simply because:
- You disagree with the opinion expressed
- You don't like the music style
- You're in a personal dispute with the creator
- The content is controversial but doesn't violate guidelines

## Account Security

### Protecting Your Account

- Use a strong, unique password
- Enable two-factor authentication (when available)
- Don't share your account credentials
- Log out on shared devices
- Report suspicious activity immediately

### Compromised Accounts

If your account is compromised:
1. Change your password immediately
2. Review recent activity
3. Contact support
4. Report any unauthorized content
