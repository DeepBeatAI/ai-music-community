# User Safety Guide

## Staying Safe on the AI Music Community Platform

Your safety and well-being are our top priorities. This guide provides tips and best practices for staying safe while using the platform.

## Account Security

### Creating a Strong Password

**Do:**
- Use at least 12 characters
- Include uppercase and lowercase letters
- Include numbers and special characters
- Use a unique password for this platform
- Consider using a password manager

**Don't:**
- Use common words or phrases
- Use personal information (birthdate, name)
- Reuse passwords from other sites
- Share your password with anyone
- Write passwords in unsecured locations

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

When available, enable two-factor authentication:
- Adds an extra layer of security
- Requires a second verification step when logging in
- Protects your account even if your password is compromised

### Account Access

**Best Practices:**
- Log out when using shared or public devices
- Don't save passwords on public computers
- Review login activity regularly
- Change your password if you suspect compromise
- Enable login notifications (when available)

### Recognizing Phishing Attempts

Be cautious of:
- Emails asking for your password or personal information
- Links to fake login pages
- Messages claiming your account will be deleted
- Requests for payment or financial information
- Suspicious attachments or downloads

**Remember:** We will never ask for your password via email or message.

## Privacy Protection

### Personal Information

**What to Keep Private:**
- Full name (unless you choose to share)
- Home address
- Phone number
- Email address
- Financial information
- Social security number or ID numbers
- School or workplace details
- Family member information

**What's Safe to Share:**
- Artist name or stage name
- General location (city/region)
- Music genre preferences
- Creative interests
- Professional music credentials

### Privacy Settings

Review and adjust your privacy settings:
- Control who can see your profile
- Manage who can contact you
- Set visibility for your content
- Control notification preferences
- Manage data sharing options

### Profile Information

**Tips for Safe Profiles:**
- Use a stage name instead of your real name
- Avoid sharing identifying details in your bio
- Don't include contact information in public fields
- Be cautious with profile photos
- Consider what information you want public

## Interacting Safely

### Communicating with Others

**Safe Communication Practices:**
- Keep conversations on the platform
- Don't share personal contact information
- Be cautious of requests to move to other platforms
- Trust your instincts about suspicious behavior
- Report inappropriate messages

**Red Flags:**
- Requests for personal information
- Pressure to share contact details
- Inappropriate or sexual messages
- Requests for money or financial help
- Threats or intimidation
- Attempts to isolate you from the community

### Meeting People Offline

**Important Safety Rules:**

If you decide to meet someone from the platform in person:
- Meet in a public place
- Tell someone where you're going
- Bring a friend if possible
- Have your own transportation
- Keep your phone charged
- Trust your instincts

**We Recommend:**
- Keep relationships online initially
- Get to know people over time
- Be cautious of people who rush relationships
- Never feel pressured to meet in person

### Recognizing Manipulation

Be aware of:
- **Love bombing**: Excessive flattery or attention
- **Isolation**: Attempts to separate you from friends
- **Gaslighting**: Making you doubt your perceptions
- **Guilt-tripping**: Making you feel responsible for their feelings
- **Pressure**: Rushing you into decisions or actions

## Content Safety

### Consuming Content

**Protect Yourself:**
- Be aware of content warnings
- Take breaks if content is disturbing
- Report content that violates guidelines
- Block users who post harmful content
- Seek support if content affects you negatively

**Content to Be Cautious Of:**
- Graphic or violent content
- Content promoting self-harm
- Disturbing or shocking material
- Content that triggers negative emotions
- Misinformation or conspiracy theories

### Creating Content

**Safe Content Creation:**
- Think before you post
- Consider how content might be perceived
- Avoid sharing personal information in content
- Be mindful of your digital footprint
- Remember that content can be screenshot or shared

**Avoid:**
- Posting when emotional or upset
- Sharing content you might regret later
- Including identifiable locations in real-time
- Revealing sensitive personal details
- Creating content under pressure from others

## Dealing with Harassment

### Recognizing Harassment

Harassment includes:
- Repeated unwanted contact
- Threatening or intimidating messages
- Spreading false information about you
- Sharing your personal information (doxxing)
- Sexual harassment or unwanted advances
- Coordinated attacks from multiple users

### Immediate Actions

If you're being harassed:

1. **Don't Engage**: Don't respond to the harasser
2. **Document**: Take screenshots of harassment
3. **Block**: Block the user immediately
4. **Report**: Report the harassment using the report button
5. **Seek Support**: Talk to someone you trust

### Reporting Harassment

When reporting harassment:
- Provide specific examples
- Include screenshots if possible
- Explain the pattern of behavior
- Note if you feel unsafe
- Request urgent review if needed

For detailed reporting instructions, see the [User Reporting Guide](guide-user-reporting.md).

### After Reporting

While waiting for moderator review:
- Keep the user blocked
- Don't retaliate or engage
- Continue documenting if harassment persists
- Reach out to support for urgent situations
- Take care of your mental health

## Protecting Minors

### If You're Under 18

**Additional Safety Rules:**
- Never share your age or school
- Don't share photos that show your face clearly
- Be extra cautious about meeting people offline
- Tell a trusted adult if something makes you uncomfortable
- Report any inappropriate contact immediately

**Red Flags for Minors:**
- Adults asking personal questions
- Requests for photos or videos
- Attempts to keep conversations secret
- Offers of gifts or opportunities
- Pressure to do things you're uncomfortable with

### If You're a Parent or Guardian

**Protecting Young Users:**
- Discuss online safety regularly
- Monitor their platform usage
- Know who they're interacting with
- Encourage open communication
- Report concerning behavior immediately

**Warning Signs:**
- Secretive behavior about online activity
- Receiving gifts from unknown sources
- Emotional changes after using the platform
- Reluctance to discuss online friends
- Spending excessive time on the platform

## Mental Health and Well-Being

### Healthy Platform Use

**Maintain Balance:**
- Set time limits for platform use
- Take regular breaks
- Don't compare yourself to others
- Remember that content is often curated
- Prioritize real-life relationships

**Signs of Unhealthy Use:**
- Neglecting responsibilities
- Sleep disruption
- Anxiety when not using the platform
- Negative impact on mood
- Isolation from offline relationships

### Dealing with Negative Experiences

**If You're Feeling Upset:**
- Take a break from the platform
- Talk to someone you trust
- Engage in offline activities you enjoy
- Practice self-care
- Seek professional help if needed

**Resources:**
- Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
- National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 1-800-273-8255
- SAMHSA National Helpline: 1-800-662-4357

### Supporting Others

If someone shares concerning content:
- Take it seriously
- Encourage them to seek help
- Report content promoting self-harm
- Don't promise to keep secrets about safety
- Contact authorities if someone is in immediate danger

## Copyright and Legal Safety

### Respecting Copyright

**Protect Yourself Legally:**
- Only upload content you have rights to
- Get permission for samples or covers
- Understand fair use limitations
- Credit sources appropriately
- Respond promptly to copyright claims

**Consequences of Violations:**
- Content removal
- Account restrictions
- Legal action from copyright holders
- Permanent ban for repeated violations

### AI-Generated Content

**Legal Considerations:**
- Disclose AI-generated content
- Understand AI tool terms of service
- Respect training data copyrights
- Don't claim AI content as entirely human-created
- Stay informed about evolving AI regulations

## Scams and Fraud

### Common Scams

**Be Aware Of:**
- Fake collaboration opportunities
- Requests for upfront payment
- Too-good-to-be-true offers
- Phishing for account credentials
- Fake record label or agent contacts
- Investment or money-making schemes

### Protecting Yourself

**Red Flags:**
- Requests for money or financial information
- Pressure to act quickly
- Promises of guaranteed success
- Requests for personal information
- Poor grammar or spelling in "official" messages
- Offers that seem too good to be true

**If You Suspect a Scam:**
- Don't send money or information
- Don't click suspicious links
- Report the user or content
- Contact support
- Warn others if appropriate

- Threats of violence or harm
- Child safety concerns
- Harassment or bullying
- Sharing of personal information (doxxing)
- Scams or fraud attempts
- Content promoting self-harm
- Illegal activities

1. Click the **report button (ðŸš©)** on the content

**If Someone is in Immediate Danger:**
- Call local emergency services (911 in US)
- Report to platform support immediately
- Provide as much information as possible
- Don't wait for platform response in emergencies
